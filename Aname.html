About Our Institution


St.Gabriel’s Sr. Sec. School was established on 6th July, 1959 and is managed by the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel. It has class Nursery to 12th in English medium. St. Gabriel’s Sr. Sec. School is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi under the Affiliation No. 1030259. The institution is established and run by the Christian minority community, mainly meant for the members of the community but open to everyone .Preference will be given to Catholics. Admission is subject to vacancies. St. Gabriel’s Sr. Sec. School, Jabalpur is under the management of the Society of the Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel, Whose headquarter is at Bhopal.
